The Process

Your 12-Step Publishing Pathway

From contract to global publication — a clear, structured journey with expert guidance at every stage.

Contracting

Agree dates, sign contract and 50% deposit to secure your publication date.

Manuscript Submission

Submit manuscript with author questionnaire. Reviewed immediately.

Cover Design

2–3 creative directions presented. Together you refine until you love it.

Cover Sign Off

Approve the final cover as ready for print and global publication.

Editing

Your editor strengthens clarity and flow. Optional developmental edit available.

Proofreading

Professionally proofread and checked by a second proofreader for accuracy.

Manuscript Sign Off

Review and approve your final manuscript as ready for typesetting.

Typesetting

Professionally typeset for paperback with optional hardback edition.

Kindle Formatting

Formatted for Kindle ebook distribution on all digital devices worldwide.

Audiobook Creation

Professional virtual voice audiobook. Option to record in your own voice.

Publication Sign Off

Review the final typeset book and give approval for worldwide publication.

Global Publication

Published worldwide in paperback, Kindle and Audiobook from day one.
