The Process

Your 12-Step Publishing Pathway

From contract to global publication — a clear, structured journey with expert guidance at every stage.

Contracting

Agree submission and publication dates. Sign contract and 50% deposit to secure your publication date.

Manuscript Submission

Submit your manuscript with author questionnaire. Reviewed immediately to confirm everything is ready.

Cover Design

Your designer creates 2–3 creative directions. Together you refine until you have a cover you're proud of.

Cover Sign Off

You approve the final cover design as ready for print and global publication.

Editing

Your editor strengthens clarity and flow. Optional developmental edit for deeper structural guidance.

Proofreading

Professionally proofread and checked by a second proofreader for accuracy and polish.

Manuscript Sign Off

Review and approve your final manuscript as ready for typesetting.

Typesetting

Professionally typeset for paperback with optional hardback. Now it truly looks and feels like a real book.

Kindle Formatting

Formatted for Kindle ebook distribution, reading perfectly on digital devices worldwide.

Audiobook Creation

Prepared for audiobook production using a professional virtual voice. Option to record in your own voice.

Publication Sign Off

Review the final typeset book and approve it as ready for worldwide publication.

Global Publication

Published worldwide in paperback, Kindle and Audiobook. Your book is now available globally.

The Package

Everything Included

The standard PublishU publishing package covers a 30,000-word book published in three formats — paperback, Kindle and audiobook — everything you need to professionally publish worldwide.

Optional enhancements include hardback edition, human-voice audiobook, additional word count, premium developmental editing, illustrations, infographics, language translation, and 60-day fast-track publication.
